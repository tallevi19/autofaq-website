How It Works

Three simple steps to verify every customer at the point of sale

1

Trigger at Checkout

When an age-restricted product is added to the cart, AgeGuard automatically prompts staff to verify the customer's age before proceeding.

2

Verify the Customer

Staff enter the customer's date of birth into the intuitive date picker. AgeGuard instantly confirms whether they meet the required age threshold.

3

Complete the Sale

The sale proceeds seamlessly. The verification is automatically logged, the order is tagged, and a record is kept for your compliance audit trail.
