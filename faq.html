📚 General

AutoFAQ is an AI-powered Shopify app that automatically generates FAQ sections for your product pages. It reads your product data — title, description, variants, tags, and more — and uses AI to create relevant, natural-sounding question-and-answer pairs that help customers make informed purchase decisions.
AutoFAQ is built for Shopify merchants of all sizes — from solo founders launching their first store to large brands managing thousands of products. If you want to improve product page conversions, reduce customer support tickets, and save hours of manual writing, AutoFAQ is for you.
Yes, AutoFAQ is currently built exclusively for Shopify and is available through the Shopify App Store. It integrates natively with your store's product data and storefront using Shopify's APIs and metafields.
AutoFAQ uses state-of-the-art large language models from leading AI providers including OpenAI and Anthropic. These models are designed to understand product context and generate accurate, helpful, and human-sounding FAQ content.

🚀 Getting Started

Install AutoFAQ directly from the Shopify App Store. Click "Add app," follow the authorization prompts, and you'll be ready to generate your first FAQ in minutes. No credit card required to start with the Free plan.
Setup takes less than 5 minutes. After installing, simply open AutoFAQ from your Shopify admin, select a product, and click Generate. Your first FAQ section can be live on your storefront within minutes.
No coding required. AutoFAQ is designed for merchants, not developers. The entire experience — generating, editing, customizing, and publishing FAQs — is done through a simple point-and-click interface inside your Shopify admin.
AutoFAQ works with all Shopify themes, including Online Store 2.0 themes. FAQs are stored as Shopify metafields and rendered using native Liquid blocks, ensuring seamless integration with any theme without custom code.
AutoFAQ requests only the permissions it needs to function:
  • read_products — to read your product data for FAQ generation
  • write_products — to save generated FAQs back to your products
  • read_metaobjects / write_metaobjects — to store FAQ content as Shopify metafields
No payment or customer data is ever accessed.

🔧 Technical

AutoFAQ reads the following product data from your Shopify store to generate contextually relevant FAQs:
  • Product title and description
  • Variants (sizes, colors, materials, etc.)
  • Product tags
  • Product type and vendor
The more detailed your product descriptions, the more accurate and useful your generated FAQs will be.
FAQs are stored directly in your Shopify store as product metafields (and metaobjects). This means the data lives in your Shopify account — not on a third-party server — ensuring reliability, portability, and compliance with Shopify's data standards.
No. FAQ generation happens inside the AutoFAQ admin panel, not on your storefront. On the storefront, FAQs are rendered as static Liquid HTML — there are no external API calls at load time, so there is zero performance impact on your page speed.
Yes. AutoFAQ is fully compatible with Online Store 2.0 themes. It uses Shopify's native section and block architecture, so you can add your FAQ section anywhere on a product page using the theme editor — no code changes needed.
AutoFAQ collects minimal data necessary to operate the app: your Shopify store URL, access tokens for API calls, the product data used for generation, and usage statistics (e.g., number of generations). No customer data, payment information, or personally identifiable information is stored. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
Product data is sent to AI providers (OpenAI / Anthropic) solely for the purpose of generating your FAQs. This is necessary for the AI generation to work. We do not sell your data or share it with any other third parties. You can review our full data practices in the Privacy Policy.
