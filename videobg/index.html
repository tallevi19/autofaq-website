How it works

Up and running in
four simple steps

1

Install the app

Add Scurix Video Background from the Shopify App Store to your store in one click.

2

Enable the embed

Open your theme editor, navigate to App embeds, and toggle Scurix Video Background on.

3

Add a video

Paste a YouTube, Vimeo, or TikTok URL, choose the page and placement type, and save.

4

Go live

Your video is live on your storefront. Enable, disable, or edit any time from your dashboard.

Video types

Choose how video appears

Four distinct placement modes for every use case in your store.

Background

Plays behind an existing section — the section's content and text stay on top. Perfect for hero banners.

Replace element

Swaps a page element entirely for the video player. Great for product showcase sections.

Full-page backdrop

Fixed behind all page content, covering the entire viewport. No CSS selector needed.

Inline embed

Inserts a responsive 16:9 player directly above the target element as a content block.
